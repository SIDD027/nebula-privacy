Nebula App Privacy Policy (Development Version)

Effective Date: 05/09/2025

1. Introduction

Nebula App (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store your data during development and testing of the app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Instagram Account Information: username and user ID obtained via Instagram OAuth for login.
  • User Content: posts, comments, and media uploaded to the app.
  • Usage Data: optional metadata such as timestamps for posts or interactions.

We do not collect personal emails, passwords, or other private information beyond what you provide.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users via Instagram.
  • To display posts, comments, and media inside private circles.
  • To moderate content and maintain community safety.

We do not share your data with third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All collected data is stored securely in our database.
  • Media files are stored on secure cloud storage.
  • Data can be deleted upon user request.

5. User Rights

  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
  • You can revoke Instagram access through your Instagram account settings.
